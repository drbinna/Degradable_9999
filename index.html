About the brand

A creative recycling brand built around people, culture, and the planet.

Our brand exists to reimagine waste as a resource. By using discarded materials that would otherwise pollute the environment, we create furniture and utility artworks that carry meaning, beauty, and purpose.

Our goals

Built for impact, made by hand.

Create Employment

We train and employ young people in unconventional, creative furniture making so they can build practical skills and earn income.

Promote Recycling

We reduce ecological waste by converting non-degradable materials into durable furniture and utility artworks.

Celebrate African Design

We promote African themes, patterns, and cultural expression in furniture, interior design, and exterior decoration.

Products

Furniture with a second life.

Each piece is designed to be practical, artistic, and environmentally responsible — from statement chairs to tables, stools, outdoor pieces, and decorative installations.
